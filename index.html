StuyVesments wants to teach students about avoiding liabilities, investing in assets, and starting businesses.

Mission Statement

This club will introduce members to public and personal finance through research and entrepreneurship projects. The goal is to prepare students for the real finance world by teaching them how to maximize their wealth and assets.

A world of opportunties

Participants will have opportunities to network with guest speakers from Stuy alumni, NYU, Columbia, and other institutions. The club will also host competitions and projects based on entrepreneurship and stock trading to give members genuine experience in the world of finance.
